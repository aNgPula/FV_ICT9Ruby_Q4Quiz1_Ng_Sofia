Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator


The BMI Calculator is used for classifications to evaluate a person based on their weight and height using the units kilogram per squared meters or kg/m².

Instructions !

  1. Input your weight in Kilograms.
  2. Input your height in Meters.
  3. Press the button to calculate the BMI.
  4. The output and classification will be put in the bottom
